Systemic Risk Signals

  • Market Volatility: Implied/Realized Volatility (>= 30% / 3000 bps)
  • Price Divergence: DEX/CEX price feed divergence (>= 1.0% / 100 bps)
  • Bridge Net Outflow: Rapid liquidity exits (>= $5M / hour)
  • Stablecoin Depeg: Safe assets deviating from peg (>= 50 bps)
